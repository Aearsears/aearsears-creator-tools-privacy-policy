# 🎬 Aearsears Creator Tools

## What It Does

Aearsears Creator Tools is a personal automation application designed to streamline the publishing of YouTube Shorts to your YouTube channel. It simplifies the content distribution workflow, allowing creators to efficiently manage and publish their short-form video content.

## Who Uses It

This application is designed for content creators and YouTubers who produce and manage YouTube Shorts. It's ideal for creators looking to automate their publishing workflow and maintain better control over their content distribution strategy.

## Google APIs Accessed

> **YouTube Data API** - Access, manage, and publish videos to your YouTube channel
>
> **Google OAuth 2.0** - Securely authenticate your Google account and obtain necessary permissions

The application requests only the minimum scopes necessary to publish Shorts to your channel. No additional user data beyond what is required for publishing is collected or accessed.
